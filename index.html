Submission Process

The length of the papers needs to be contained between two (2) and six (6) pages, including figures, tables, and references.

Papers need to be blinded and should follow the template provided above.

For the final submission, the papers should reviewed by addressing the comments of the different reviewers. Do not forget to unblind the papers.

The papers will be send by email to chair of the Scientific Committee (Cansen Jiang) in PDF format.


Revision Process

Each participant at the Doctoral Day will be assigned at least one paper to review.

The members of the Scientific Committee will assign the reviewer for each paper. They will as well review supplementary materials.

The Scientific Committee strives for a double blind review.
