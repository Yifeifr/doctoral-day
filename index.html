What is the LE2I Doctoral Day!

The 2nd LE2I Doctoral Day will offer a unique experience to BSc, MSc and PhD candidates to present and share there experience.

The symposium has been revisited and will take the form of a collegiate conference following the 3C rule:

Community, Collaboration, Continuity

The Doctoral Day in a nutshell
